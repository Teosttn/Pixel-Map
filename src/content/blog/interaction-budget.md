## Motion should have a job

Pixel effects are easy to overuse. The rule for this site is simple: motion should reveal structure, confirm interaction, or make the world feel alive in the background.

## Reduced motion is part of the design

The canvas background and page transitions respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Visitors who ask for a calmer interface still get the same content and navigation.

## The durable layer

The durable layer is the content: posts, projects, updates, and the about page. Animation supports it, but does not become the thing people came to read.
