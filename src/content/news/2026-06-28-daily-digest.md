English summary: A recurring product pattern: AI systems become more useful when they gain stable surfaces for editing, verification, and handoff.

中文短评：重点不是聊天会不会消失，而是结构化工作区会在哪些场景成为默认入口。

English note: The interesting question is not whether chat disappears, but where structured workspaces become the default.
