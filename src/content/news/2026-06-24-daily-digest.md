## 1. 容器优先布局比视口优先布局更稳定 / Container-first layouts feel calmer than viewport-first layouts

中文摘要：容器查询让可复用组件根据父容器适配，而不是依赖整个视口宽度做猜测。

English summary: Container queries make reusable components adapt to their parent instead of guessing from the full viewport.

中文短评：这对仪表盘、卡片和需要在不同网格之间移动的内容工具尤其有用。

English note: Especially useful for dashboards, cards, and content tools that move between grids.
